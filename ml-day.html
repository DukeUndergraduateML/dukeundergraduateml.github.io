Duke Machine Learning Day

March 31, 2018 | Gross Hall, Duke University

Schedule

  • 9am–9.45am: Registration
  • 9am–9.45am: Breakfast
  • 10am–10.45am: Keynote
  • 11am–11.45am: Research spotlight
  • 12pm–1.30pm: Networking lunch
  • 1pm–3pm: Panel discussions
  • 3pm–3.30pm: Refreshments
  • 3.30pm–5.45pm: Workshops
  • 5pm–6.30pm: Poster session
  • 5.30pm–6.30pm: Dinner

Come to Duke University's first Machine Learning Day on Saturday, March 31, 2018 in Gross Hall! During this exciting event, you will have the opportunity to:

  • Participate in panel discussions from upperclassmen exploring careers in machine learning post-graduation, as well as professors and graduate students pursuing research in machine learning to learn what graduate-life is like;
  • Take part in workshops in areas such as deep learning and neural networks, object recognition, data cleansing, and more; and
  • Watch research and poster presentations and a keynote on ML-related research.
More information will be posted soon. Companies can sponsor the event by sponsoring Duke Undergraduate Machine Learning.
